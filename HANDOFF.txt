WHAT WE WERE BUILDING
---------------------
A Strategic Synthesis tab inside the Business Intelligence section of the app.
The goal: take all research outputs (science, regulatory, market, competition)
and produce a PRESCRIPTIVE business strategy - not just analysis, but recommendations.
This was the missing piece identified in the architecture review.

The synthesis produces 6 sections:
  1. Situation Assessment       - honest 3-5 sentence summary of where we stand
  2. Commercial Strategy        - specific recommended pathway with 2-3yr horizon
  3. Critical Near-Term Decisions - 3-5 timeboxed decisions (Decision|Rec|Why format)
  4. Investment Thesis          - 2-3 sentences an investor would say to a partner
  5. Risk-Adjusted Path         - concrete fallback if primary strategy hits obstacle
  6. What We Must Prove in 12M  - 3 measurable proof points to de-risk next raise

WHY IT GOT STUCK
----------------
File writing on this Windows machine has two bugs:
  1. The Write tool fails with EEXIST when the parent directory already exists.
     (It tries to mkdir the parent even if it is there.)
  2. Bash heredoc (python3 << PYEOF) crashes when the Python content inside
     contains triple-single-quoted strings like Python docstrings.
     Bash misparses them as shell delimiters.

WORKAROUND THAT WORKS
---------------------
Use python3 -c with a list of lines joined at the end.
Avoid triple-quoted strings entirely - build content line by line.
See llm_client.py for an example that was written this way successfully.
